What I Do

K-POP

K-Pop refers to Korean popular music and includes globally renowned idol groups like BTS, Blackpink, and EXO. K-Pop is known for its catchy melodies, impressive choreography, and visually appealing music videos. The culture of engaging with fans, known as "fandom culture," is also a significant aspect of K-Pop.

K-DRAMA

Korean dramas are popular for their emotional storylines, excellent acting, and high production quality. Recent hits like "Crash Landing on You," "Itaewon Class," and "Sweet Home" have gained international acclaim through global streaming platforms like Netflix. K-Dramas span various genres, including romance, thriller, and fantasy, and often reflect cultural elements and everyday life in Korea.

K-FOOD

Korean cuisine is gaining popularity worldwide, with dishes like kimchi, bibimbap, bulgogi, and tteokbokki being favorites among many. The influence of the Korean Wave (Hallyu) has led to an increased interest in trying Korean food. Both traditional and fusion Korean dishes are appreciated for their unique flavors and cooking methods, appealing to people who enjoy healthy and delicious meals.
